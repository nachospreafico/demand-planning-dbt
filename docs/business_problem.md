\# Business Problem



\## Background



Demand planning teams rely on data from multiple operational systems, including forecasts, sales, inventory, and product master data. These datasets are often inconsistent, duplicated, or modeled differently across reporting tools.



As a result, analysts spend significant time preparing data instead of generating insights.



\## Problem Statement



Business stakeholders require trusted and standardized metrics for:



\- Forecast Accuracy

\- Inventory Health

\- Service Level

\- Product Performance



Without a centralized transformation layer, different teams may calculate the same KPI differently, reducing trust in the data.



\## Objective



Build a production-style analytics engineering pipeline using dbt that transforms raw operational data into analytics-ready models.



\## Expected Outputs



\- Fact tables for sales, inventory, and forecasts

\- Dimension tables for products, stores, and dates

\- Data quality tests

\- Automated documentation and lineage

\- Executive dashboard for business users
